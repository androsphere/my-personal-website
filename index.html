About Me

Audio Engineer from Leadville to Denver

I grew up in Leadville, Colorado, where my parents and my brother instilled in me an interest in music, video games, and jokes. My schooling fueled interest in math as well as more diverse genres of music.

While attending Lake County High School, it dawned on me that a career choice was necessary. My passions for gaming and music helped me determine that I would only be truly happy in a career that involved either, or both!

I graduated from the University of Colorado Denver in May of 2018 with a bachelor's of science in Recording Arts. Since 2024 I have been a Broadcast Engineer with Price Productions applying my technical knowledge to ensure quality livestreaming audio and video.
